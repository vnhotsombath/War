How to play:

In this classic card game, the player (you) will play against a computer.

♡The deck of 52 cards is shuffled each round so that both players get a random card.

♢The game starts once you hit the draw button. The 52 deck is split evenly so each player will start off with 26 cards.
Whoever has the highest card out of the two wins that round and takes the opponent's cards.
2 is the lowest value card, Ace is the highest value card

♤When there is a 'tie' both players deal 4 cards,
the last card is put face down and whoever has the highest card, winner takes all.

♧First player to 52 wins, or if one player reaches 4 or less cards, they automatically lose.
